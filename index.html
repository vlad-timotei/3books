version 3.0 in romanian and english.
  • lightbulb_outline Watch carefully both images and try to remember, without checking in the Bible, what book is in between. Also, note the clue above! When you find the solution, click on the colored letters, one by one, in the correct order.
  • loopIf you accidentally clicked a letter, click it again and it will be erased. You can also delete everything you typed by clicking the reset button.
  • error_outlineIf your solution is incorrect, you will have to try again. If you think that's too difficult, click on home and select the easy game mode - you will get the clue sooner and the letters are alphabetically ordered.
  • helpAfter a guess or two, depending on the game mode, you will be able to get a clue - the first letter of the word.
  • assignment_turned_inAfter you enter the correct solution you go to the next level.
